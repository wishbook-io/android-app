About Us

Wishbook Info Services Pvt Ltd’s vision is to digitize the apparel / fashion production & sales information, enabling tools that lead to efficiency for traditional Apparel and Fashion Manufacturers, Distributors & Retailers. Our learnings come from years of technology / product development & running traditional textile manufacturing & e-Commerce companies.

Wishbook Catalog App allows managing the complete B2B sales process – from from Manufacturers to Distributors / Wholesalers to Retailers, including sharing of catalogs, orders and order status, accounts status and sales force management.
