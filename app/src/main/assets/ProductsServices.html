Products & Services

Product Offering

WishBook key product offering is a B2B Catalog / Sales App, which does digital catalog sharing from manufacturers to distributors to wholesalers to retailers, and enables salespersons to show catalogs, take orders & file daily reports. This mobile & web app will replace the printed catalog books that are expensive & slow to share. Faster feedback on the styles & their movement will allow the manufacturer to better plan repeat production.

Key components of the B2B Catalog / Sales App are:

  1. Relationships between Merchants or Users, as Buyers and Suppliers.
  2. Sharing of Catalog releases, with differential pricing and availability.
  3. Placing Sales or Purchase Orders, with the Seller updates on the Orders.
  4. Payment statuses and reminders.
  5. Salesperson management and reporting.
  6. Counter or Shop-in-shop inventory management.
  7. Integrations with various Accounting and Product Lifecycle managment softwares.
